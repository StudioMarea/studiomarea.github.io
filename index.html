About

Photographing the space between wild and tender.

I split my life and lens between the misted forests of the Pacific Northwest and the green coastlines of Costa Rica. That distance shapes how I see — a search for honest light, raw texture, and the calm that lives inside a moment.

Whether it's a glacier-fed river, an expecting mother, or a resident of the rainforest canopy, my approach stays the same: unhurried, natural, and devoted to the subject.

— Marea
